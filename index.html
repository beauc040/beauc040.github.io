Mild cases of H1N1 novel influenza virus continue to appear in Minnesota   May 8, 2009
To date, the Minnesota Department of Health has identified 16 probable and two confirmed cases of the H1N1 novel influenza in Minnesota. All of the cases so far have been mild. More severe cases will likely appear as MDH focuses its testing on hospitalized cases.

Influenza in Swine   May 7, 2009
While forms of influenza are present in pigs in Minnesota, the exact type that has been making people sick, H1N1 virus, has not been found in pigs any where in the world. This H1N1 hybrid virus has never been identified in swine.

2009-10 Minnesota Legislative Manual online   May 11, 2009
Minnesota Secretary of State Mark Ritchie announces the availability of the 2009-2010 Minnesota Legislative Manual edition online. This biennially published book, commonly referred to as the "Blue Book," is available to view and download.

Minnesota World Trade Week 2009   May 11, 2009
Minnesota World Trade Week is an annual event organized by the Minnesota Trade Office to acknowledge and celebrate the importance of international trade to the state's economy. This year's Minnesota World Trade Week will feature the Governor's Global Trade Conference offering an impressive lineup of policymakers and business leaders who will offer their insight and guidance for enhancing the state's global competitiveness.
